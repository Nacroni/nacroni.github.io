How to turn your videos into crap!

Created on 8/9/2023

Here is a tutorial on how to make your videos look like inconcievable garbage!

  • y2mate to download videos to 3GP

  • CloudConvert to convert and compress videos to MP4 mayhem

Downloading videos via y2mate

    If you want to download TikTok videos instead use SnapTik, but you will have to skip to CloudConvert and then convert the videos MP4 to MP4 instead.
  • Paste a link or enter a search query to find your video of you choosing.
  • Once selected, download it as "144p (.3gp)".

Converting videos via CloudConvert

    Upload your video to CloudConvert, and then select the Wrench icon. Choose these options:

    Video:

  • Set Resolution to custom 72 x 72 (it'll revert back to 16:9)

  • set it to 51 (worst quality)

  • set Preset to ultrafast

    • Audio:

  • set Audio Bitrate to 1

  • set Channels to mono

  • set Sample Rate to 8000
